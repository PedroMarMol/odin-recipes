Scrambled eggs

Description

This one is such a simple and easy way to make a quick breakfast or brunch.

You can also add some spices so everytime you cook it, it tastes different.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs.
  • 6 tbsp of full cream milk.
  • A knob of butter.
  • Salt.

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs with the milk and add a bit of salt until it's consistent.
  2. Heat a small pan and after a minute add the butter.
  3. Let it sit for 10 seconds or so, then fold it over (repeat this until the eggs are soft).
